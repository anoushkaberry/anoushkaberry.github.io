About me

I graduated in May 2021 with a BS in Mathematics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I specialized in Operations Research. This means that I took classes focusing on optimization and problem solving. The degree requirements included computer science and statistics courses such as Numerical Methods (using Python & the library NumPy) and Statistical Modeling in R. One of the more interesting math courses I took was called Computational Mathematics and included projects in Crpytography, data analysis, graph algorithms and computer algebra among other topics. Apart from this, I completed my liberal arts education with a wide variety of courses ranging from Introduction to Shakespeare to Society & the Brain. I love to bake, play the piano, watch TV & front-end development. During summer in 2020, I worked for a start-up for whom I created a website which was followed by working for the University museum focusing on their website and front-end developement. I was awarded a place on the LAS Dean's List for Spring 2020 and Fall 2020 for scoring in the top 20 percent of the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences.
